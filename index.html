GANGS

Ballas

The Ballas are one of the oldest African-American gangs in Los Santos. Before 2013, they took control of Grove Street
and wage their usual wars with the Families. The iconic color of the Ballas is the same color worn by the Ballas of the HD universe, purple. Each member
wears at least one purple garment on them that color is common to the city's baseball and basketball teams, the Los Santos Panic Basketball Team and the Boars Baseball Club.






Marabunta

Weazel News reveals that the gang is at war with a Hispanic gang called the Aztecas for drug trafficking control and that a shootout
has recently taken place between the two gangs in the Grand Senora Desert. The gang is also involved in arms trafficking. The members of
the Marabunta Grande are easily identifiable by their tattoos on their faces and their clothes and bandanas are cholos style. The main color used by the gang is blue.






Cartel Des Madrazo

The Madrazo Cartel or Madrazo Cartel, or simply The Cartel is an American-Mexican drug cartel led by Martin Madrazo in Bost City.






Vagos

The Vagos are recognized as one of the most violent gangs in all of San Andreas. They are predominant on the east side of the city of Los Santos. They are the rivals of the Varrios and the Grove Street Famillies.






Families

Of all the gangs in the State of San Andreas, they are considered the most honorable gang,
because of their opposition to drugs despite the other crimes they commit. The Grove Street Families are also one of the oldest gangs in Los Santos.

It is a gang whose principles are fundamental, and all members must respect them. There are three types of Grove Street Families members that the player may spot:

The first member has dreadlocks, a green checkered shirt and beige pants;
The second wears a green bandana, a green sweater, classic jeans and also white shoes;
and the third has a cap, a green tank top, black jeans, and black and white shoes.
